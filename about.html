About Our Project

I was UI Designer -- 2; UI Designer -- 1 was tasked with building a different representation of the same site. I was given color's, font's, and a logo to work with. The rest of the project I had to come up with on my own. I chose to go with a mobile first design as most users are now visiting you from a mobile site rather than visiting you from a computer or desktop site.

We started out with a team of 5 who did not know each other plus a Project Leader. We were given 4.5 days and were tasked with building a multi level site from different frameworks. You can see a bit more about the 5 people who worked on this project and their roles by clicking on the tabs above.
